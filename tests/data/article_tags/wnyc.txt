      <div class="article-description">
        <p>This week Alec sits down with actor Stacy Keach.  Some fans know Keach for his portrayal of Hamlet and Falstaff; others recall him as Sergeant Stedanko in Cheech and Chong’s <em>Up in Smoke</em>. </p>
      </div>
      <div class="article-description">
        <p><a href="http://www.wnyc.org/shows/heresthething/2013/may/27/transcript/" target="_blank"><strong>READ | Full Transcript</strong></a></p>
<p>Stacy Keach has been acting for more than half a century and as he explains to Alec, his philosophy on the craft has evolved: “I think in my early days I started pretty much from the outside and tried to get a fix on what the character looked like and …  I think it’s better to start inside and work out if you can.”  </p>
